Hello! I’m Caitlin. I’m a collaborative writer and a keen story editor.

I work across formats, including half-hour comedy, one-hour drama, TV movies, feature film and adaptation. There is always friendship, adventure, and a coming of age journey. Aren’t those the best parts of life? I write stories that center on community and the desire to belong in worlds that evoke the mysterious and the intimate. My tone ranges from feral, to funny, to full of heart. My worldview has been shaped through a female lens as a bi/queer person. I am driven by a deep interest in people, and why they do what they do.

I spent a decade traveling, working odd jobs, and figuring out how to write. My formal training and early career was in theatre – specifically in collective creation, which inspired me to pursue TV writing. I spent two years doing stand up and three years deeply involved in Toronto’s improv community. During this time I also co-wrote and produced online sketches with Arms Up Comedy and wrote scripts for immersive escape games.

Fun personal fact: I have a toe thumb. Yes, it makes bowling difficult.
