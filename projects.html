in development

fight the moonlight

Eager to fit in an adventurous New Girl accepts a terrifying dare in the woods, only to be attacked by a mysterious creature. Looking for answers she teams up with a courageous Outcast to investigate who – or what – did this to her, uncovering the town’s horrific secret – the curse of loup garou.

FIGHT THE MOONLIGHT is a YA horror musical feature film.

the narrows

A seventeen-year-old Jr. Ranger returns to work in Narrows Park – the same woods her twin sister went missing 10 years ago. Sure beyond a doubt that her sister is still alive, she incites the help of other Jr. Rangers to uncover the park’s secretive history, and the supernatural forces connected to the land.

THE NARROWS is a coming of age one-hour anthology TV series.

grace

GRACE is a dark comedic web series about a girl at odds with a puppet version of herself.

disorderlies

When Detroit’s uninsured citizens come knocking, two hospital orderlies, a med school dropout and an immigrant with an unrecognized medical degree, decide to play doctor and perform back alley surgeries for patients in need.

DISORDERLIES is a half hour dramedy that explores a Robin Hood-esque approach to a tragically broken system.

the grownies

Four childhood friends return to their hometown to finish a quest they failed as kids.

THE GROWNIES is a serialized one-hour adventure TV series.
